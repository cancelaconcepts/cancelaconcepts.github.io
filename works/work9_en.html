Magazine Illustration - Will it be Fashion? She knows

This project was developed in an academic context of Drawing IV, in Instituto Politécnico do Cávado e do Ave. The objective was to do an magazine illustration, in which an article documents a woman named Lidewij Edelkoort who predicts the future tendencies of fashion. The concept behind the illustration was to characterize the author spoken in this article through the astral cards, named Major Arcana, which each card has a different and significant meaning:
- Card 0 - The Mad - Predictions of the future;
- Card VII - The Car - Sucess, triunph and travels (in the article it says she travels a lot);
- Card XIV - The Temperance - Harmony and hope (in spite of not having a direct meaning with the characteristics of the author, it intends to represent the fashion and beauty subject because of the figure that this card portrays).
