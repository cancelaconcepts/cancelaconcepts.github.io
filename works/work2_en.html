Museu Etnográfico de Chavão

This project was first developed in an academic context of Project IV, in Instituto Politécnico do Cávado e do Ave, and then became a personal project, where I could test my 3D capabilites. The objective was to develop the graphic identity of an cultural company/organização. The chosen organization was "Museu Etnográfico de Chavão". It is located in one of the parishs from Barcelos, named Chavão, in Portugal. This museums holds and exhibits objects from the rural culture of Chavão, showing it's history and the traditional costumes. The project was divided in four phases:
- The development of the logo;
- The development of the signs used in the museum;
- The development of the stationery/supports;
- The development of the manual of graphic rules.
