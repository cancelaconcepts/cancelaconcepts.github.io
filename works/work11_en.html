Photomontage - Alice in Wonderland: Dormouse

This project was developed in an academic context of Digital Drawing I, in Instituto Politécnico do Cávado e do Ave. The objective was to choose a character from the story Alice in Wonderland and interpret an excerpt of the tail in which the character is included, using Adobe Photoshop. The chosen character was the Dormouse, and the chosen excerpt was the dinner, in which the Dormouse, Alice, Mad Hatter and the March Hare were participating. In this dinner, the Dormouse was always sleeping while the other characters were talking. After moments of talking, Mad Hatter and March Hare decide to pickup the Dormouse an put him inside a teapot, waking him up instantly. This is my interpretation of the Dormouse chock.
